Coffee already fuels everything from first dates to business meetings, but what if it could also build community? Grounded is an initiative focused on transforming leftover coffee grounds into an affordable, sustainable material. Rather than discarding pounds and pounds of used grounds every day, coffee shops can give back to the communities they serve and help them flourish.
